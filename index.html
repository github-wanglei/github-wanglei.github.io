Biography

Wang Lei, Ph.D., is a young distinguished professor of Soochow University, a young science and technology lifting talent (provincial young talent) of Jiangsu Association for Science and Technology, an executive member of CCF Pervasive Computing Committee, an executive member of CCF Internet of Things Special Committee, and a member of SIGBED China. In April 2020, he received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University, which was fully funded by the China Scholarship Council and jointly trained by the University of New South Wales (QS World Ranking 19th). From September 2020 to November 2022, he worked as an assistant researcher at the School of Computer Science, Peking University. His main research interests are artificial intelligence, Internet of Things, wireless intelligent perception, etc. He has published more than 20 papers, including 9 papers (7 CCF A, 2 CCF B) as the first author, including top conferences and journals such as MobiSys, UbiComp, TMC, etc., and has won the UbiComp Outstanding Paper Award of CCF A conferences, WASA Best Paper Award of CCF-C conferences, and the Rising Star Award of the International Computer Society (ACM) (Jiangsu Province). Serve as a PC Member or Track Chair of important academic conferences such as ICDCS, HPCC, ICA3PP, WASA, etc. As the only person in charge of a domestic unit (Publicity Chair), he participated in the organization of MobiQuitous 2022, a well-known conference in the field of ubiquitous computing. He has served as a reviewer for many international first-class journals such as UbiComp (CCF-A conference), ACM CHI (CCF-A conference), Acta Electronica Sinica (CCF-A Chinese journal), IEEE TWC (CCF-B journal), ACM TOSN (CCF-B journal), etc. He has presided over four general and youth projects of the National Natural Science Foundation of China and provincial and departmental projects, and participated in a number of projects funded by the Baidu Foundation of Peking University and the research projects of Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd., and the relevant research results have been transformed and implemented in Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. He instructed undergraduates to participate in national A-class competitions such as the China Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Competition and the Global Campus Artificial Intelligence Algorithm Elite Competition, and won the first prize in the national competition.

    VECTOR: Velocity Based Temperature-field Monitoring with Distributed Acoustic Devices
    Haoran Wan, Lei Wang, Ting Zhao, Ke Sun, Shuyu Shi, Haipeng Dai, Guihai Chen, Haodong Liu, Wei Wang
    ACM IMWUT (UbiComp) 2022, Sep 2022.

    We introduce VECTOR, a temperature-field monitoring system that achieves high temperature sensing accuracy and fast response time using commercial sound playing/recording devices.


    LoEar: Push the Range Limit of Acoustic Sensing for Vital Sign Monitoring
    Lei Wang, Wei Li, Ke Sun, Fusang Zhang, Tao Gu, Chenren Xu, Daqing Zhang
    ACM IMWUT (UbiComp) 2022, Sep 2022.

    We propose LoEar, which is a novel acoustic sensing system using only a single microphone and speaker to detect vital signs (respiration and heartbeat) with a significantly increased sensing range (> 6 meter).


    Unlock With Your Heart: Heartbeat-based Authentication on Commercial Mobile Phones
    Lei Wang, Kang Huang, Ke Sun, Wei Wang, Chen Tian, Lei Xie, Qing Gu
    ACM IMWUT (UbiComp) 2018, October 2018.

    ''Unlock with your heart'' uses the built-in accelerometer to capture the heartbeat signals on commercial mobile phones. The user only needs to press the phone on his/her chest, and the system can identify the user within a few heartbeats.
